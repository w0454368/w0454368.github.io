How to Play

Start the game by guessing a letter and typing it into the guess field. Guesses are not case-sensitive. In order to win the game, the player must guess the hidden word that is generated at the start of the game, with blanks being provided as characters to give the player the number of characters in the word. Each time the player guesses a correct letter, the blank space will be filled in by the corresponding letter. If the player guesses an incorrect letter, "hangman" object gets added. Player loses the game if they get "hangman".
